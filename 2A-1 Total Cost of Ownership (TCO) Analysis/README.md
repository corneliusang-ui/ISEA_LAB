# 2A-1 Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) Analysis

This folder contains materials and analysis related to Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) calculations and evaluations.

## Overview

Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) is a comprehensive financial estimate that includes all direct and indirect costs associated with the acquisition, implementation, and maintenance of a system, product, or service over its entire lifecycle.

## Contents

- Documentation and analysis files related to TCO assessments
- Tools and methodologies for calculating total costs
- Case studies and examples
